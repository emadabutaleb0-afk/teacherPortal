# Egyptian 3rd Secondary English Curriculum 2025-2026

## Official Units (from Ministry textbook)

### TERM 1

**Unit 1: Inside the World of Care**
- Lesson 1: My Day in the Life of a Nurse (Reading/Vocabulary)
- Lesson 2: A Day in the Life of a Doctor (Listening/Speaking)
- Lesson 3: Tired Hands, Caring Hearts (Grammar: Present Perfect Simple Active & Passive)
- Lesson 4: Writing – Formal Letter / Report on Healthcare
- Lesson 5: Vocabulary & Functions – Expressing Opinions on Healthcare

**Unit 2: The Power of Machines**
- Lesson 1: Reading – Machines That Changed the World
- Lesson 2: Vocabulary – Technology & Machines
- Lesson 3: Grammar – Passive Voice (Simple Present & Past)
- Lesson 4: Listening & Speaking – Discussing Technology
- Lesson 5: Writing – Argumentative Essay on Technology

**Unit 3: Protect Nature, Protect Future**
- Lesson 1: Reading – Environmental Issues
- Lesson 2: Vocabulary – Environment & Nature
- Lesson 3: Grammar – Conditionals (1st & 2nd)
- Lesson 4: Listening & Speaking – Climate Change Discussion
- Lesson 5: Writing – Persuasive Essay on Environment

**Unit 4: Marine Life**
- Lesson 1: Reading – Oceans and Sea Creatures
- Lesson 2: Vocabulary – Marine Vocabulary
- Lesson 3: Grammar – Relative Clauses
- Lesson 4: Listening & Speaking – Marine Conservation
- Lesson 5: Writing – Descriptive Essay on Marine Life

**Unit 5: From Muscles to Mindset**
- Lesson 1: Reading – Sports and Mental Health
- Lesson 2: Vocabulary – Sports & Fitness
- Lesson 3: Grammar – Modal Verbs (Ability, Advice, Obligation)
- Lesson 4: Listening & Speaking – Sports Discussion
- Lesson 5: Writing – Opinion Essay on Sports

**Unit 6: Let's Hang Out!**
- Lesson 1: Reading – Social Activities & Free Time
- Lesson 2: Vocabulary – Leisure & Entertainment
- Lesson 3: Grammar – Reported Speech
- Lesson 4: Listening & Speaking – Making Plans
- Lesson 5: Writing – Informal Letter / Email
